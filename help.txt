      ACTIVITY TRACKING
 - '$weeklyActivity' sends the weekly activity leaderboard

 - '$allTimeActivity' sends the all-time activity leaderboard

 - '$trackMe' asks the bot to start tracking your activity in the discord

 - '$poggyBoard' displays the poggy point leaderboard

 - '$poggyBalance <name>' displays someone's poggy point total
